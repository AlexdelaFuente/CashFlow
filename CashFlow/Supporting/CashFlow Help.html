Welcome to CashFlow!

CashFlow is designed to help you manage your finances effectively by tracking your expenses and income. It offers a variety of ways to visualize and filter your financial data, all within an easy-to-use and visually appealing app for iOS

Getting Started

  • Entering the App: Either sign up or register a new account if you don't have one.
  • Initial Setup: Begin by setting up your first transactions to populate the charts and lists.

Using the Application

  • Adding Transactions:

  • All Transactions:
    • Apply filters to find specific transactions. You can filter by date (ascending or descending), type (income/expense), payment method (card or cash), category, date range, amount range and description using the search bar located at the top.
  • Charts:
    • Analyze your balance, monthly summaries, and expenses through various charts.
